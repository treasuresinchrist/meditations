---
title: "Christ Our Life"
---
> **"When Christ, who is our life, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in glory."**  
> — Colossians 3:4

The Scriptures present the Lord Jesus Christ as every aspect of the believer's life.

---

## 1. Christ — The Source of Our Life

**Ephesians 2:1**

> **"And you hath he quickened, who were dead in trespasses and sins;"**

**Galatians 2:20**

> **"I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me: and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me."**

---

## 2. Christ — The Sustenance of Our Life

**John 6:51**

> **"I am the living bread which came down from heaven: if any man eat of this bread, he shall live for ever: and the bread that I will give is my flesh, which I will give for the life of the world."**

---

## 3. Christ — The Solace of Our Life

**Hebrews 13:5**

> **"...for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee."**

---

## 4. Christ — The Object of Our Life

**Philippians 1:21**

> **"For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain."**

---

## 5. Christ — The Pattern of Our Life

**Philippians 2:5**

> **"Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus:"**

---

## 6. Christ — The Glory of Our Life

**1 John 3:2**

> **"Beloved, now are we the sons of God, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is."**

---

Christ is:

- The Source of our life.
- The Sustenance of our life.
- The Solace of our life.
- The Object of our life.
- The Pattern of our life.
- The Glory of our life.

A believer's life begins with Him, is sustained by Him, is comforted in Him, is occupied with Him, is shaped after Him, and will finally be glorified in His presence.
