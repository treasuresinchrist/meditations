---
title: Together with Christ
date: 13-07-2026
---
One of the greatest truths of the New Testament is that the believer is united with Christ. We are not just forgiven by Him or helped by Him. We have been brought into living union with Him.
The New Testament unfolds these wonderful truths.

**1. Planted Together:**

_"For if we have been planted together in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection." - Romans 6:5_

A believer's new life begins at the cross.
When Christ died, God saw us identified with Him. Our old man has been crucified with Christ. Sin no longer has the right to reign over us.

**2. Quickened Together:**

_"...even when we were dead in sins, hath quickened us together with Christ..." - Ephesians 2:5_

Dead sinners cannot make themselves alive. God gave us the very life of the risen Christ. The life we now possess is a new life, a resurrection life.

**3. Raised Together:**

_"And hath raised us up together..." - Ephesians 2:6_

We have not merely been given life. God has raised us out of our old position. We belong to a new creation. We have a new standing before God.

**4. Seated Together:**

_"...and made us sit together in heavenly places in Christ Jesus." - Ephesians 2:6_

Though still on earth physically, our position is already in heaven. Our citizenship, acceptance and every spiritual blessing are all found in Christ.

**5. Suffer Together:**

_"...if so be that we suffer with him..." - Romans 8:17_

Union with Christ does not exempt us from suffering. Rather, it explains it. The world rejected Him. It will also reject those who belong to Him.

**6. Heirs Together:**

_"...heirs of God, and joint-heirs with Christ..." - Romans 8:17_

We are not merely saved from judgment. We have become joint-heirs with Christ. We are the children and heirs of God.

**7. Glorified Together:**

_"...that we may be also glorified together." - Romans 8:17_

A believer's life does not end with suffering. It ends with glory.
The One with whom we died, now live, and suffer is also the One with whom we shall reign forever.

**Our life with Christ -**

1. Planted together - **We died with Christ.**
2. Quickened together - **We live with Christ.**
3. Raised together - **We are raised with Christ.**
4. Seated together - **We are seated with Christ.**
5. Suffer together - **We suffer with Christ.**
6. Heirs together - **We inherit with Christ.**
7. Glorified together - **We reign with Christ.**

Our Christian life is inseparably linked with Christ. We died with Him, live with Him, have been raised and are seated with Him, suffer with Him, inherit with Him, and will one day be glorified with Him. 

From the cross to the crown, God has joined us to His Son. What is true of Christ as the risen Man before God has, by grace, becomes true of every believer through union with Him.

What a wonderful Saviour! What a wonderful salvation!
