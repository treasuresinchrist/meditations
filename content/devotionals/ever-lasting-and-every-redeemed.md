---
title: Everlasting and Ever-Renewed
---

> **"O give thanks unto the LORD; for He is good: for His mercy endureth for ever."**
>
> Psalm 136:1

> **"It is of the LORD'S mercies that we are not consumed, because His compassions fail not. They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness."**
>
> Lamentations 3:22–23

God's mercy shines in a precious twofold glory: it is **everlasting**, and it is **ever-renewed**.

The psalmist repeats again and again, **"for His mercy endureth for ever"** (Psalm 136), reminding us that His mercy never weakens, never expires, and never ceases to flow towards His people.

Yet this enduring mercy is not distant or static. Scripture also tells us, **"They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness."** The mercy that endures forever is the same mercy that rises afresh with each sunrise—renewed, refreshed, and perfectly suited to the needs of each new day.

Thus, the believer stands upheld by a mercy that is both **infinite and intimate**—enduring forever in its nature and ever-renewed in its expression; ancient as eternity, yet fresh as the morning light.


**Truly, the mercy of our God is fresh each morning, yet unchanging forever.**
