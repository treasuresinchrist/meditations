---
title: Christ's Ability
---
The Scriptures repeatedly declare what the Lord Jesus Christ is **able** to do for His people. His ability is unlimited because it rests upon His divine power, His finished work, and His unchanging priesthood.

---

## 1. Able to Save
**Hebrews 7:25**

> **"Wherefore he is able also to save them to the uttermost that come unto God by him, seeing he ever liveth to make intercession for them."**

---

## 2. Able to Keep
**Jude 24**

> **"Now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy,"**

---

## 3. Able to Succour
**Hebrews 2:18**

> **"For in that he himself hath suffered being tempted, he is able to succour them that are tempted."**

---

## 4. Able to Do Exceeding Abundantly
**Ephesians 3:20**

> **"Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us,"**

---

## 5. Able to Make Grace Abound
**2 Corinthians 9:8**

> **"And God is able to make all grace abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work:"**

---

## 6. Able to Subdue All Things
**Philippians 3:21**

> **"Who shall change our vile body, that it may be fashioned like unto his glorious body, according to the working whereby he is able even to subdue all things unto himself."**

---

## 7. Able to Present Us Faultless
**Jude 24**

> **"Now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy."**

---

The Lord Jesus Christ is able:

- To save us completely.
- To keep us securely.
- To succour us tenderly.
- To do exceeding abundantly.
- To make all grace abound toward us.
- To subdue all things unto Himself.
- To present us faultless before His glory.

Our confidence rests not in our ability, but in **His**.
