---
title: Lord Jesus Christ - The King
---

## Lord Jesus is the:

## 1. King of Glory

> “Who is this King of glory? The LORD of hosts, he is the King of glory. Selah.”  
> — Psalm 24:10

He is the revealed manifestation of God, clothed in divine majesty and triumph.

---

## 2. King of All the Earth

> “For God is the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.”  
> — Psalm 47:7

His authority is universal — no land, people, or power lies outside His rule.

---

## 3. King of Jacob

> “Produce your cause, saith the LORD; bring forth your strong reasons, saith the King of Jacob.”  
> — Isaiah 41:21

He is the covenant King who governs, defends, and contends for His people.

---

## 4. King of Israel

> “Thus saith the LORD the King of Israel, and his redeemer the LORD of hosts; I am the first, and I am the last; and beside me there is no God.”  
> — Isaiah 44:6

He is Israel’s sovereign Redeemer — eternal, self-existent, and unmatched.

---

## 5. King of Heaven

> “Now I Nebuchadnezzar praise and extol and honour the King of heaven, all whose works are truth...”  
> — Daniel 4:37

He is the One who rules from heaven, overruling every earthly throne.

---

## 6. King of the Jews

> “Art thou the King of the Jews? And Jesus said unto him, Thou sayest.”  
> — Matthew 27:11

Rejected by men, yet owning His kingship even in suffering.

---

## 7. King of the Nations

> “Who would not fear thee, O King of nations? or to thee doth it appertain...”  
> — Jeremiah 10:7

All are accountable to Him — the True King, whether they acknowledge Him or not.

---

## 8. King of Righteousness

> “To whom also Abraham gave a tenth part of all; first being by interpretation King of righteousness, and after that also King of Salem, which is, King of peace.”  
> — Hebrews 7:2

His rule is founded on perfect righteousness — unchanging and eternal.

---

## 9. King of Peace

> “To whom also Abraham gave a tenth part of all; first being by interpretation King of righteousness, and after that also King of Salem, which is, King of peace.”  
> — Hebrews 7:2

True peace flows from the righteousness established by His reign.

---

# He is Crowned With:

## 1. Crown of Thorns

> “And the soldiers platted a crown of thorns, and put it on his head, and they put on him a purple robe.”  
> — John 19:2

---

## 2. Glory and Honour

> “But we see Jesus, who was made a little lower than the angels for the suffering of death, crowned with glory and honour...”  
> — Hebrews 2:9

---

## 3. Crown of Fine Gold

> “For thou preventest him with the blessings of goodness: thou settest a crown of pure gold on his head.”  
> — Psalm 21:3

---

## 4. Crowns of Silver and Gold

> “Then take silver and gold, and make crowns, and set them upon the head of Joshua...”  
> — Zechariah 6:11

---

## 5. Many Crowns

> “And on his head were many crowns; and he had a name written, that no man knew, but he himself.”  
> — Revelation 19:12

---

Rejected on earth, the Lord Jesus Christ was crowned with thorns.

Exalted in heaven, He is crowned with glory and honour.

He is:

- The **King in humility** — born in a manger.
- The **King in suffering** — lifted upon a cross.
- The **King in triumph** — reigning at God’s right hand.

He is the King who saves by His blood, reigns in righteousness, and will soon return for us.

**Worthy is the Lamb that was slain.**
