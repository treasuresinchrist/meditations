---
title: Christ - The Mighty to Save
---
**Mark 5**

In Mark 5, the Lord Jesus is revealed as the **Mighty to Save**, displaying His power over every consequence of sin.

---

## 1. From the Power of Sin
**Demons — Mark 5:1–20**

The man possessed by a legion of demons is delivered by the authority of Christ. He who was bound by Satan is found "sitting, and clothed, and in his right mind."

---

## 2. From the Plague of Sin
*(Its consequences in a fallen world)*

**The Woman with the Issue of Blood — Mark 5:25–34**

The woman who had suffered for twelve years is instantly healed by touching the Lord in faith. Her affliction was not presented as the result of personal sin, but as one of the sorrows brought into the world through sin. Christ proves Himself sufficient for the deepest need and the longest suffering.

---

## 3. From the Penalty of Sin
**Jairus' Daughter — Mark 5:21–24, 35–43**

The dead child is raised by the voice of the Lord Jesus. He who conquered disease also conquered death itself.

---

Christ is revealed as:

- A Saviour for **man, woman, and child**.
- A Deliverer from **the devil, disease, and death**.
- Mighty to save from:
    - The **power** of sin.
    - The **plague** of sin *(its consequences in a fallen world)*.
    - The **penalty** of sin.

> **"Thou shalt call his name JESUS: for he shall save his people from their sins."**  
> — Matthew 1:21
